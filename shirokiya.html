Shirokiya


Located underground at the Ala Moana Mall sits the Shirokiya village walk, which is a food court generally focused on food mixtures between Hawaiian, French, and Japanese followed by an indoor beer garden. Their main operation concept is known as omotenashi no kokoro (the Japanese philosophy of hospitality). From cooking performances to Ennichi (festival days) events, Shirokiya will give a feeling of nostalgia and excitement to people of all ages worldwide.
