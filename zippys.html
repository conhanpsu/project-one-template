Zippy's


If you are in a hurry during your vacation and need to have some Hawaiian food on the go, Zippy's is perfect for that. With many delicious selections to choose from, you have the option to do take-outs so you can bring dinner during family gathering. Or if you prefer, Zippy's also have a whole section in their restaurant for dine-ins if you'd like to have the full table service experience!
